Detect fake news from Articles

Analyze news articles or links in real-time using our advanced AI. Our platform checks the content against trusted sources, evaluates its credibility, and provides a detailed report with confidence scores. Ensure the news you consume is factual and reliable with our cutting-edge tools.

Detect fake news from Images

Verify the authenticity of images associated with news stories. Our platform uses AI-powered image analysis to identify signs of manipulation and cross-references visual data with verified sources. Stay informed by uncovering the truth behind misleading visuals.

GPS based Local verification

GPS-Based Local Verification LiveTruth enhances news accuracy with GPS-based local verification. This feature sends SMS requests to people in the specific location of a news event, allowing them to confirm or deny the reported information. By leveraging GPS, we ensure that local news is verified firsthand, improving the credibility of regional stories and combating misinformation. This adds an essential layer of verification, fostering trust and transparency in real-time news.

Detect fake news from Live Broadcasting

LiveTruth uses advanced AI to detect fake news in real-time during live broadcasts. By analyzing the content through our fine-tuned model, we assess its authenticity and generate a confidence score. This enables broadcasters and viewers to identify misinformation as it happens, ensuring transparency and promoting accurate reporting. Our solution helps in maintaining the credibility of live events, empowering users to make informed decisions while combating the spread of fake news.
